About Us.

We're dragon lovers, just like you. Here at dragons for you, we train, feed and care for our dragons from egg to adulthood. We believe the key to a good dragon is vigorous training and superb feeding.

To ensure the best lineages for our dragons, we get our eggs from reputable breeders: our centuries-long partnership with the traditional Valyrian Dragonlords has, for years, helped us provide our clients with the strongest and biggest dragons Westeros has ever seen.
